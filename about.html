About Us

Our Story

Talsma Furniture has 4 retail store locations in West Michigan. Our vast selection of quality vendors make it possible for us to offer a huge selection of style, comfort, and value for any budget. Most of our vendors even offer special orders, letting you express your unique sense of style.

We take great pride in our unique shopping experience and dedication to excellent customer service. As a family owned business, our employees are part of the family too. We believe in treating our customers like guests in our home. You are invited to browse at our stores without the fear of a pushy salesman following your every move. Relax, come on in and you will see why we are different!

Our History

Talsma Furnitureâ€™s beginnings started in 1948 in Hudsonville, with Bert Talsma and his father. Bert had spent some time working at other furniture stores and helping his dad with farming before getting into the furniture business himself. At first the small store carried everything from appliances to gifts, and later focused primarily on furniture.

There were some bumps along the way, including a fire that destroyed the Hudsonville location and forced the Talsma family to open a location in Holland in 1984. When the Hudsonville store was finally rebuilt, the Talsma's focused on growing the chain, and on the Grand Rapids area. After several additions to both Hudsonville and Holland, the family decided to open a third location in Byron Center in 1999 just off US 131 south of Grand Rapids. In April of 2013, a fourth location was opened in Grand Rapids (Cascade).
